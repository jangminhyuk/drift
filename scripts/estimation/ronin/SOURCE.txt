model_resnet1d.py is the RoNIN ResNet-1D architecture from the RoNIN project
(Herath et al., "RoNIN: Robust Neural Inertial Navigation"). It is vendored
here only to load the IMU encoder used by the adaptive module. See the official
RoNIN repository for the source and its license, and download the pretrained
checkpoint from there (place at checkpoints/ronin/checkpoint_gsn_latest.pt).
